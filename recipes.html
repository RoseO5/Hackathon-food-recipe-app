Chicken and Rice Casserole

Ingredients:
  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1 can cream of mushroom soup
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas and carrots
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:

Preheat oven to 350°F. Mix all ingredients in a bowl. Transfer to a greased casserole dish. Bake for 25-30 minutes until heated through. Let stand for 5 minutes before serving.

Grilled Chicken Salad

Ingredients:
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 6 cups mixed greens
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/4 red onion, sliced
  • Olive oil and lemon dressing
Instructions:

Season chicken with salt, pepper, and olive oil. Grill for 6-7 minutes per side until cooked through. Let rest for 5 minutes, then slice. Arrange salad greens and vegetables on plates. Top with sliced chicken and drizzle with dressing.

{% elif first_food == 'Fish' and second_food == 'Rice' %}

Teriyaki Salmon with Rice

Ingredients:
  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1/2 cup teriyaki sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds
  • 2 green onions, sliced
Instructions:

Heat sesame oil in a pan. Add salmon fillets and cook for 4-5 minutes per side. Brush with teriyaki sauce during the last 2 minutes of cooking. Serve over rice, garnish with sesame seeds and green onions.

{% elif first_food == 'Eggs' and second_food == 'Tomatoes' %}

Shakshuka

Ingredients:
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 cups chopped tomatoes
  • 1/2 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh parsley
Instructions:

Heat olive oil in a skillet. Add onions, bell pepper, and garlic. Cook until soft. Add tomatoes, paprika, and cumin. Simmer for 10 minutes. Make wells in the mixture and crack eggs into them. Cover and cook for 5-7 minutes until eggs are set. Garnish with parsley.

{% elif first_food == 'Beef' and second_food == 'Beans' %}

Texas Chili

Ingredients:
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 2 cans kidney beans
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Salt and pepper
Instructions:

Brown ground beef with onions and garlic. Drain excess fat. Add beans, tomatoes, and spices. Simmer for at least 30 minutes, preferably 1-2 hours. Adjust seasoning and serve hot.

{% elif first_food == 'Prawns' and second_food == 'Rice' %}

Prawn Fried Rice

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup cooked rice (preferably day-old)
  • 1/2 lb prawns, peeled and deveined
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
Instructions:

Heat oil in a wok or large skillet. Scramble eggs and set aside. Add prawns and cook until pink. Add rice and break up clumps. Add vegetables and cook until tender. Return eggs to wok. Add soy sauce and mix well. Stir in green onions and sesame oil before serving.

{% elif first_food == 'Sardines' and second_food == 'Bread' %}

Sardine Toast

Ingredients:
  • 1 can sardines in olive oil
  • 4 slices bread, toasted
  • 1/2 avocado, mashed
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • Fresh dill or parsley
Instructions:

Toast bread until golden. Mash avocado with lemon juice and spread on toast. Drain sardines and place on top of avocado. Arrange red onion slices over sardines. Garnish with fresh herbs and a drizzle of the sardine oil.

{% elif first_food == 'Beans' and second_food == 'Rice' %}

Cuban Black Beans and Rice

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup dried black beans (or 2 cans)
  • 1 cup rice
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 green pepper, chopped
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Oregano
Instructions:

Cook beans according to package directions. Sauté onion, pepper, and garlic in olive oil. Add cooked beans, cumin, and oregano. Simmer for 15 minutes. Cook rice separately. Serve beans over rice.
